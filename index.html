About Us

We are Cal Poly Humboldt's community of data enthusiasts, bringing together students from all majors who share a passion for data science.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced programmer, the Data Science Club offers a welcoming space to learn, collaborate, and grow. We explore machine learning, data visualization, statistical analysis, and real-world applications through hands-on projects and workshops.
